    How to Use

    Enter the letters you got from your Wordle game:

    You can click anywhere on the grid to start typing in your letters.

    On the computer, right click any letter to change the state of the letter to match your Wordle game.

    On mobile, double click any letter to change the state of the letter to match your Wordle game.

    States:

    • Green Letters – Letters you know are correct at that position.
    • Yellow Letters – Letters you know are wrong at that position but are found in the word.
    • Grey Letters – Letters you know are not in the word at all.

    Click Analyze to find all possible words. You’ll see the probability of guessing correctly and clicking the results shows the list of candidate words.

    Use this tool to narrow down your guesses and maximize your chances.
